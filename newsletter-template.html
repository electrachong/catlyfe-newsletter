Finally, a platform for your cat
Did you know cats want to express themselves too? Many cat minions have tried to address this by creating social profiles for their furry overlords on Facebook, Twitter, and other social media websites designed for humans.

Catlyfe is a new microblogging service being developed just for cats to express themselves to their heart's content.

The steps are simple!
1. When your cat approaches your keyboard, indicating they would like to fulfill their daily quota of social media activity, you will be able to click an easy button to start a new catpost.
2. Place your cat on the keyboard and watch it craft a post in the mysterious cat web language.
3. When your cat seems satisfied with its post, hit Publish!
We aim to translate for you :3
Cats definitely know what they are doing when they pad all over the keyboard, but that doesn't mean it's easy to decipher what they write. Catlyfe is working to develop cutting-edge algorithms to translate the meaning of catposts into human-intelligible content.
